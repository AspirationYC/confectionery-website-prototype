About

Founded in 2008 by Jon Beau. When Jon Beau was 6 years old, he loved eating candies just like any children. However, his parents couldn't afford to buy candies to him everyday. His parents him bought candies every months. When, he got older, he dreamed to start a candy shop industry and let every children have a chance to eat candies everyday so he made the price very cheap.
